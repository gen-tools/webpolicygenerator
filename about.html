About WebPolicyGenerator

Making Legal Compliance Simple, Transparent, and Accessible

We believe that every website owner should have access to professional legal documents without the complexity and expense traditionally associated with legal services.

Our Mission

To democratize legal compliance by providing free, high-quality legal document templates that help website owners protect their businesses and respect user privacy.

Legal Protection

Provide comprehensive legal protection through well-crafted policies that comply with modern privacy regulations.

Always Free

Keep our service completely free, with no hidden fees, premium plans, or upgrade pressure. Forever.

Privacy First

Respect user privacy by processing everything client-side with no data collection or tracking.

Our Privacy Approach

We practice what we preach. Our commitment to privacy isn't just in the documents we generate—it's built into how our platform works.

No Data Collection

We don't collect, store, or track any personal information. Everything happens in your browser.

Client-Side Processing

All document generation happens locally in your browser. Your information never leaves your device.

No Tracking

No analytics, no cookies, no fingerprinting. We respect your digital privacy completely.

Open Source

Our code is open source, so you can verify our privacy claims and contribute to improvements.

Open Source & Transparent

Transparency builds trust. That's why our platform is open source and our processes are completely transparent.

Community Driven

WebPolicyGenerator is built by the community, for the community. Our open-source approach means you can:

  • Review and verify our code
  • Contribute improvements and fixes
  • Suggest new features and templates
  • Help translate for global accessibility

Join our growing community of developers, legal professionals, and privacy advocates working together to make the web more compliant and transparent.
